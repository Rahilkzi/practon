1.If…then…Else:
Q.1. Write a PL/SQL block to display number is even or odd from given number:

declare
x int;
begin
x:=&x;
if mod(x,2)=0 then
dbms_output.put_line('Even Number');
else
dbms_output.put_line('Odd Number');
end if;
end;
